About Me

Hi, I’m Donato! A passionate aspiring game developer with a love for programming and technology. Over the past few years, I’ve developed a deep interest in computer science, which I pursued alongside my previous job as a waiter. This unique combination has fueled my learning journey and motivates me to continue growing as a developer.

I’ve always had a fascination with how things work behind the scenes, whether it’s a game, a website, or an application. The challenge of creating something from scratch and watching it come to life is what drives me in my learning journey.

Outside of programming, I enjoy gaming, immersing myself in a wide variety of music, and spending time with my friends. I believe that a balance between work and hobbies is crucial for staying motivated and energized.

I'm now looking for an opportunity in the IT sector where I can continue to grow professionally, refine my skills, and contribute to exciting projects. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and collaborate in team-oriented environments.
